--------
Physics
--------

Regarding the physics, some standard notation for the neutron transport is considered.

.. math::
   :nowrap:

    \begin{align*}
    & \Psi := \text{Angular Neutron Flux} \\
    & \Phi := \text{Scalar Neutron Flux} \\
    \end{align*}


-------------------------------------
Differential and Algebraic Operators
-------------------------------------


The differential operators will be written with 

.. math::
   :nowrap:

    \begin{align*}
    & \mathcal{L}
    \end{align*}

and 

.. math::
   :nowrap:

    \begin{align*}
    & L
    \end{align*}

-------------------------------------
Iteration Indices.
-------------------------------------

We use the following conventions for the indices inside the loops:

.. math::
   :nowrap:

    \begin{align*}
    & n := \text{general iteration index,} \\
    & l := \text{source iteration index,} \\
    & i,j := \text{spatial dofs indices,} \\
    & q := \text{quadrature index,} \\
    & f := \text{face index,} \\
    & c := \text{cell index,} \\
    & m := \text{moments index,} \\
    & n := \text{angles index,} \\
    & g := \text{energy group index,} \\
    & d := \text{dimension index.}
    \end{align*}

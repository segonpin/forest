Scattering from group g to h for phi added to source.

This is the main function of the matrix free. It works at group level, i.e., it gives

\begin{align*} Q_h = Q_h + S_{g \to h} \Phi_g \end{align*}

Note
When you want to get just the scattering coming from group g, then you must provide the vector \( Q_h \) set to zero, ( \( Q_h = 0 \)). Instead, in general you will provide the vector \( Q_h \) filled with the fission source, or with the outer scattering,
\( Q_h = \sum_{g'\neq g} S_{g' \to g} \Phi_{g'} + F_{g}\Phi_{g} \).
